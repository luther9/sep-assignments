**Using proper pseudo-code, describe three or more primitive algorithms used in
  your day-to-day life.**

**Calculating a tip**

SET subtotal to sum of item prices
SET percentage to tax + tip_percentage
RETURN subtotal * (1 + percentage)

**Getting to work**

IF raining
  get in car
ELSE
  get on bike
ENDIF
WHILE not at work
  go to next intersection
  go whichever way is closest to work
ENDWHILE

**Replacing batteries**

IF device not working
  turn off device
  open battery compartment
  take out batteries
  put in new batteries
  replace battery cover
  turn on device
ENDIF
